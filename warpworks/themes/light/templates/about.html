About This Site

I created ${request.registry.settings['name']} out of a shared interest in weaving, textiles, manufacturing history, and computation. I'm only a novice weaver, but as soon as I threw the first pick I knew I would want to be able to explore weaving in software.

The goal of the site is to make it possible for anyone to explore computer-aided textile design quickly and easily. It is built on top of a piece of software called PyWeaving, which is a low-level library for loading, manipulating, and rendering weave drafts.

Please keep in mind that this is a side project, and offered as a free service to the community. It may have bugs and problems!
